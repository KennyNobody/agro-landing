About Kalagra group

The Kalagra group of companies is an integrated holding of both Russian and UK companies intended to take advantage of the vertical opportunities offered in bioenergy growth, production and utilization in the Kaliningrad Region, Russian Federation.

Kalagra Limited is the UK holding company, used as the investment vehicle for investment outside the Russian Federation. Kalagra OOO is the overall Russian holding company and acts as the management company for three Russian subsidiaries: Kalagra Farm OOO, Timo Land OOO and Timo Invest OOO.

Kalagra Farm is the agricultural company dealing with the main production processes:



  • clearing and renovation of land;
  • planting and development of Miscanthus and other bioenergy crops;
  • harvesting and logistics of delivery of biomass;
  • development and management of the Miscanthus nursery and sale of rhizomes;
  • production of biofuel in cubes and pellets from Miscanthus and other biomass mainly for export and to the local market;
  • agronomy consultancy both to our own companies and ultimately to external farmers

Timo Land OOO and Timo Invest OOO are ringfenced companies intended to own or lease land to own companies and other companies in the Kaliningrad region and invest into the production of Miscanthus or other biofuel crops.
